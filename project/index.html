Origins

Folklore is the collection of cultural and traditional expressions of a region that are often learned orally and informally, passed down from one generation to the next.

Brazilian folklore is the result of a rich cultural blend of Indigenous, African, and European peoples—especially the Portuguese, who colonized Brazil.

This combination of traditions, legends, myths, and customs gave rise to a unique and diverse folklore, with distinct elements in each region of the country.
